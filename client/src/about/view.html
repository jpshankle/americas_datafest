About ImmiViz

Every year millions of people migrate around the globe for one reason or another. This type of movement can create both opportunities and challenges at macro and micro levels. Various organizations around the world collect data that can be used to better understand these challenges and opportunities, but there isn't an easy way to bring it all together.

That's where ImmiViz comes in.

We gather up all of that data, and present it to you in a visual format that is easy to understand and digest.
